OUR MISSION

Splash is a nonpartisan, 501(c)(3) nonprofit working to lead the world into a sustainable water future by aiding in the resolve of the global water crisis and striving toward water equality for all. Established in 1998, its original objective was to raise public awareness regarding the vital role water plays in our daily lives. Today, Splash has grown its vision to include investing in the next generation of water leaders, equipping informed decision makers, and inspiring water advocates across the globe.
